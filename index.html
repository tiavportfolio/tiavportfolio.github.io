WHO IS TIA?

Tia is a graphic designer based in Toronto who loves making things look good and make sense.
She works across branding, packaging, motion graphics, and the occasional poster that spirals into
a full-on design obsession. Her projects usually start with too many sketches, a strong coffee,
and a playlist that’s doing most of the emotional heavy lifting. She’s big on storytelling through visuals,
the kind that feels playful, intentional, and a little unexpected.
